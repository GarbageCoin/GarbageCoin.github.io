  • What is GarbageCoins

    GarbageCoin is a match between garbage recycling and digital currency. You will get a certain amount of GarbageCoin when you recycle your garbage to a local Garbage Recycling and Disposal Station(GRDS). The idea comes from the invention of Bitcoin.

How can I get GarbageCoins

We planned to open many Recycling Centers in living areas, say, at selected supermarkets, and Automated Recycling Machines will be designed and installed. In these centers, you can recycle a variety of waste material including cans, glass and plastic bottles, mobile phones, kitchen waste, and so on. Then a certain amount of GarbageCoin will sent to your wallet. A timely pricing system on different categories of waste materials will be designed for this project.
